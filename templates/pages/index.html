About

Our Mission

We hope to integrate multiple online shopping assistants/apps(tracking of order, expense tracking, etc.) into one unified web app so that it is a one stop experience for our end users.

  • Keep track of your expenses/spendings
  • Group buy to lower shipping cost
  • Know the status of all your deliveries
  • Platform to consolidate all your online purchases.

Shopping on E-commerce platforms has become really popular these days and we often tend to shop on multiple platforms such as Shopee, Lazada or ASOS. We can easily lose track of our total expenses or will not be able to keep track of all our delivery dates. We would have to check all platforms individually which could be a hassle! Not being able to track how much we are spending can also be dangerous and can lead to overspending. We would also love to bring costs down when shopping, and this is where ordering together with other people comes in as costs can be lowered through buying in bulk or cheaper shipping.
