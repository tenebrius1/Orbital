About

Our Mission

We hope to integrate multiple online shopping assistants/apps(tracking of order, expense tracking, etc.) into one unified web app so that it is a one stop experience for our end users.

  • Keep track of your expenses/spendings
  • Group buy to lower shipping cost
  • Know the status of all your deliveries
  • Platform to consolidate all your online purchases.

Shopping on e-commerce platforms has become very popular these days and we often tend to shop on multiple platforms like Shopee, Lazada or ASOS. In doing so, we easily lose track of our total expenses or fail to keep track of all the delivery dates. We would have to check all the platforms individually, which can be a pain! Not being able to track how much we spend can also be dangerous and lead to overspending. We'd also like to cut costs when shopping, and this is where ordering together with other people comes in, as buying in bulk or cheaper shipping can cut costs.
